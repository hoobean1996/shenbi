# Shenbi Pricing Strategy

## Overview

Shenbi operates on a three-tier business model:
1. **Direct Training** - Self-operated coding education centers
2. **B2B SaaS** - Platform licensing to other training companies
3. **School Partnerships** - Collaboration with Singapore schools

## 3. School Partnerships (Singapore)

### Pricing Models

#### Option A: Per-School Annual License

| School Size | Annual Fee (SGD) | Includes |
|-------------|------------------|----------|
| Small (<500 students) | $3,000 | Unlimited teachers & students |
| Medium (500-1,500 students) | $6,000 | + Custom reports, priority support |
| Large (>1,500 students) | $10,000 | + Dedicated account manager |

#### Option B: Per-Student Pricing

| Volume | Price/Student/Year (SGD) |
|--------|--------------------------|
| First 100 students | Free (pilot) |
| 101-500 students | $8 |
| 501-1,000 students | $6 |
| 1,001+ students | $4 |

#### Option C: MOE Bulk Licensing

For government tenders and MOE-approved programs:
- Competitive pricing based on RFP requirements
- Typically requires local education partner
- Volume discounts for multi-school deployments

### School Support Services

| Service | Price (SGD) | Description |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Teacher Training Workshop | $500/session | 2-hour hands-on training |
| Curriculum Mapping | $1,000 (one-time) | Align with MOE syllabus |
| Parent Orientation | $300/session | Introduction for parents |
| School Event Support | $300/event | Coding competitions, open house |
| MOE Compliance Audit | $2,000 | Documentation & certification |
| Quarterly Review Meeting | Included | Progress review with school |

### Pilot Program

**Free 3-Month Pilot** for qualifying schools:
- Up to 2 classes (max 80 students)
- Full platform access
- 1 teacher training session included
- Success metrics tracking
- Conversion to paid upon pilot success

## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)
- Launch direct training with 2 locations
- Validate pricing with early customers
- Collect NPS and feedback
- Refine curriculum based on data

### Phase 2: Scale (Months 6-12)
- Launch B2B SaaS platform
- Onboard first 10 training centers
- Build case studies and testimonials
- Develop school pilot program

### Phase 3: Expand (Months 12-24)
- Enter school market with pilots
- Pursue MOE partnerships
- Expand to Malaysia/Indonesia
- Consider Series A funding

## Appendix: Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring Strategy
- Always show Annual package first (highest value)
- Trial priced to convert, not profit

### Bundle Psychology
- Semester package = "best value" badge
- Annual package = "most popular" badge

### Payment Options
- Credit card (immediate)
- PayNow/PayLah (Singapore)
- Installment plans for annual packages
